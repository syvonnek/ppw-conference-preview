A focus on AI

Why AI training matters

AI is transforming how content is created, edited, and delivered — from automating repetitive editing tasks to enhancing visual storytelling with VFX and motion graphics. This program gives you the practical knowledge and hands-on skills to integrate AI into your creative process — saving time, boosting efficiency, and expanding what's possible. Workshops are led by an industry-recognized trainer from FMC and NAB Show.

Session information

Two expert-led AI sessions

Each workshop covers a specific area of AI in content creation, focused on practical, real-world application.

11:00 – 13:30

AI Video Editing — Enhance editing workflows with AI automation

For freelance and broadcast editors, particularly in news and documentary. Explore the integration of AI and machine-learning technologies in video editing with tools like DaVinci Resolve and Premiere Pro — and how these advancements can creatively enhance your work and expand your professional opportunities.

15:00 – 17:00

AI Motion Graphics & VFX — Leverage AI for advanced animation and effects

A journey through AI-enhanced VFX and motion graphics using tools like Firefly and Runway for tasks such as rotoscoping and animation — a comprehensive look at how AI streamlines post-production while emphasizing the irreplaceable value of human creativity.
